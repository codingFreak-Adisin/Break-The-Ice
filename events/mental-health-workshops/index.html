Mental Health Workshops

Break.The.Ice conducted various mental health workshops for children in underprivileged sections to empower them and help them learn more about mental health and how they can take care of themselves! We talked about mental health journals, helplines and seeking for help when you need! Around 50 children from classes 6th to 11th were enlightened and were made to realise that stigmas must not exist around mental health. With discussions and worksheets around mental health during the session, the children left the session as young mental health enthusiasts.
